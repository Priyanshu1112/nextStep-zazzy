Next chapter of zazzy

We are thrilled to announce that zazzy has officially joined the Canadian AGS Group, marking an exciting new chapter in our journey as a marketing and creative agency. This strategic partnership brings together two organizations that share a profound commitment to design thinking, innovation, and creating meaningful experiences for our clients.

At zazzy, we have always believed in the power of human-centered design and creative problem-solving. Our approach has consistently focused on understanding our clients' deepest needs, challenging conventional thinking, and delivering solutions that truly resonate with their audiences. When we discovered that Canadian AGS Group operates from the same foundational principles of design thinking, we knew this was the beginning of a powerful collaboration.

The synergy extends beyond methodology to culture. Both organizations believe in fostering environments where creativity thrives, where diverse perspectives drive innovation, and where every team member is empowered to contribute to groundbreaking solutions.

As part of restructuring, we are now proudly based in Atlanta, a thriving metropolitan area that has become a cornerstone of innovation and creativity in the Southeast. This move positions us to better serve our existing clients while opening doors to new opportunities and collaborations across diverse markets. As we embark on this exciting new chapter, we want to be transparent about the changes ahead. Effective immediately, zazzy will be focusing exclusively on developing innovative products and solutions for the internal portfolio. This strategic shift allows us to channel our creative energy into groundbreaking projects that will benefit the entire network.

Consequently, we are no longer accepting new external projects or reviewing hiring applications. Our talented team will be dedicating their expertise to creating exceptional work for our new family of companies, where we can make the most significant impact.

To our valued clients, partners, and supporters who have been part of our journey: thank you for believing in zazzy's vision. While our focus is shifting, the creative spirit and commitment to excellence you've come to expect from us will continue to flourish in this new environment.

The future is bright, and we couldn't be more excited to write this next chapter. Here's to the power of design thinking, the strength of strategic partnerships, and the endless possibilities that lie ahead.
Cheers!
Jay Mistry
CEO, zazzy
